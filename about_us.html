    About Us

    Down to Bhangra began right here in NSW with one goal: to create a space where anyone could experience the joy, power, and community of Punjabi folk dance. What started as a few passionate dancers sharing their love for Bhangra has grown into a thriving academy where tradition meets talent. From energetic classes to standout performances, we're here to keep the spirit of Bhangra alive, one beat, one step, and one smile at a time.

    Our mission is to celebrate culture, build confidence, and bring people together through the power of dance. We believe Bhangra is more than just movement, instead it's a way to connect with heritage, express yourself, and feel a sense of belonging. Our academy is proudly inclusive, welcoming dancers of all backgrounds, ages, and experience levels. Whether you're here to learn, grow, or simply feel the joy of community, we're here to uplift, empower, and support you every step of the way.

    Manbir and Jasleen started Down to Bhangra to build something that felt like home, a place where culture was celebrated and everyone felt like they belonged. Bhangra was their way of expressing joy, strength, and identity.

    So whether you're here to dance, connect, or simply try something new, you're part of the family!
