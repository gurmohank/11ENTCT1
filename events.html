    Events

    Down to Bhangra has been behind some incredible events that bring our community together. From Langar Sewa to the Kids and Ladies Mixer Party and Annual Function, we're all about celebrating culture and making memories.

    At Down to Bhangra, we proudly host an annual Langar Sewa as part of our commitment to community service and staying in touch with our roots. Each year, our team comes together to prepare and serve free meals, fostering a spirit of unity and giving back.

    Each year, Down to Bhangra brings the community together through our Ladies and Kids Mixer Night, a joyful event filled with dance, laughter, and vibrant celebration. It's our way of creating a fun, welcoming space where families can connect, unwind, and make unforgettable memories.

    Our Annual Function, Back to Roots, is a vibrant celebration of culture, talent, and community spirit. It brings together all our teams for one unforgettable night of performances, showcasing months of hard work, passion, and the joy of Bhangra in its truest form.
