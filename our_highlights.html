    Our Highlights

    Down to Bhangra's journey has been marked by some truly unforgettable moments. From dazzling audiences on Australias's Got Talent (AGT) with their high-energy performances to sharing the stage with Punjabi music sensation Diljit Dosanjh, these milestones have showcased their passion for dance and commitment to bringing Bhangra to the global stage.

    Born To Shine Tour

    In 2023, our dancers had the incredible opportunity to perform live alongside global superstar Diljit Dosanjh during his Born to Shine world tour in Sydney. From dynamic choreography to electrifying stage presence, they brought unmatched energy to one of the year's most iconic concerts — a true milestone moment for our team.

    It Was All A Dream Tour

    In 2024, our dancers lit up the stage with Karan Aujla during his It Was All a Dream tour stop in Sydney. The performance blended sharp choreography with raw energy, creating an unforgettable moment in front of a packed arena.

    Australia's Got Talent

    Our talented dancers also had the honor of showcasing their bhangra on Australia's Got Talent, where we proudly brought Punjabi culture to national television for one of the first times, captivating audiences and earning applause from judges and viewers alike.
