    Other Services

    In addition to our regular classes, we offer bespoke wedding choreography services to make your special day unforgettable. Whether you're looking for high-energy Bhangra, graceful Giddha, or the glamour of Bollywood, we tailor routines to suit your vision and skill level. Our expert instructors work closely with couples and their families to create memorable performances that will have everyone dancing with joy.

    Bhangra

    Bhangra is an energetic, vibrant dance that originates from Punjab. With its lively beats, rhythmic steps, and joyful spirit, it's all about celebration and bringing people together.

    Giddha

    Giddha is the graceful and lively counterpart to Bhangra, traditionally performed by women. It's characterized by expressive hand movements, intricate footwork, and playful storytelling that adds a touch of fun and energy to any celebration.

    Bollywood

    Bollywood dance blends traditional Indian styles with contemporary moves, creating a captivating mix of drama, elegance, and flair. It's perfect for any wedding, bringing both glamour and excitement to the stage.
